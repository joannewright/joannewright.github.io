About Me

Joanne Wright was born and raised in England with her sister--the family artist--who studied graphic design at Bath College. Despite a love of photography, color, and art supplies since childhood, it wasnâ€™t until March 2018, when a friend took her to a Howard VanBuren watercolor class, that she had the courage to paint herself. A handful of painting and sketching classes later, Joanne looks forward to further honing her skills and painting landscapes of both the USA and United Kingdom. Painting has become a passion for Joanne and she hopes her show at the Bloomfield Library will be the first of many! Joanne lives in Canandaigua with her husband of 36 years, Larry and has two grown children, Brianna, 28 and Corey, 24. She is a real estate broker/co-owner of Stargazer Property Group and CEO of Marathon Mobility, both based in Canandaigua.
